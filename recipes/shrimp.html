Spicy Grilled Shrimp Recipe

This grilled shrimp recipe is fast and easy to prepare!

Ingredients

  • 1 large clove garlic
  • 1 teaspoon coarse salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • ½ teaspoon cayanne pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 2 pounds large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 wedges lemon, for garnish

Directions

Step 1

Preheat a gas gill to medium heat (400 degrees F) and lightly oil the grate.

Step 2

Crush garlic and salt together in a small bowl with a fork. Mix in paprika and cayenne pepper.
Stir in olive oil and lemon juice to form a paste.

Step 3

Combine garlic paste and shrimp in a large bowl and toss until shrimp are evenly coated.

Step 4

Grill shrimp until opaque, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Transfer to a serving dish, garnish with
lemon wedges, and serve.
