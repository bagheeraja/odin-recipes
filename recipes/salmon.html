Slammin' Grilled Salmon Recipe

This salmon tastes so good!

Ingredients

  • 1½ pounds salmon fillets
  • lemon pepper to taste
  • garlic powder to taste
  • salt to taste
  • ⅓ cup soy sauce
  • ⅓ cup brown sugar
  • ⅓ cup water
  • ¼ cup vegatable oil

Directions

Step 1

Season salmon fillets with lemon pepper, garlic powder, and salt.

Step 2

Stir soy sauce, brown sugar, water, and vegetable oil together in a small

bowl until sugar is dissolved. Place fish in a large resealable plastic bag:

add soy sauce mixture, seal, and turn to coat. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.

Step 3

Preheat a gas gill to medium heat (400 degrees F) and lightly oil the grate.

Step 4

Place salmon on the preheated grill, and discard remaining marinade. Cook salmon until fish

flakes easily with a fork, about 6 to 8 minutes per side.
