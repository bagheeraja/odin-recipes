Easy Grilled Mahi Mahi Recipe

This grilled mahi mahi is perfect for summer!

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 3 fillets mahi mahi
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice

Directions

Step 1

Preheat a gas gill to medium-high heat (450 degrees F) and lightly oil the grate.

Step 2

Make the marinade. Combine paprika, oregano, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder,

salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper in a small bowl. Brush mahi mahi fillets with

olive oil on both sides, then brush on marinade.

Step 3

Grill on the hot grill until golden and fish flakes easily with a fork, about 4 to

5 minutes per side. Drizzle with lemon juice and enjoy!
